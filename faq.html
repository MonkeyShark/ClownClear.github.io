Content Questions

What is Clown Clear's setting?

The story of Clown Clear unfolds in a post-apocalyptic United States recently ravaged by a mysterious infection. The game consists of six different storylines, each centered on a unique character, with the goal of fending off the ravenous clown-like infected responsible for wiping out most of humanity.

Who are Clown Clear's characters?

More information on the characters will be revealed later down the road, but their names are: Noah, Jade, Sarge, Eddy, Gregory, and Charlotte.

What enemies will Clown Clear feature?

Clown Clear will feature a variety of unique special infected to fight alongside the horde.

What game modes will Clown Clear feature?

Clown Clear is planned to have multiple game modes, such as an infinite "Survival" mode, along with the standard "Campaign" mode.

Can I play Clown Clear co-operatively?

There are currently no plans for co-op in Clown Clear.

Will there be microtransactions or DLC content?

There are currently no plans for microtransactions, but DLC content has been considered.

What game engine is Clown Clear being built on?

Clown Clear is using Unreal Engine 5.

Will Clown Clear contain modding support?

Clown Clear will likely contain simple modding support, but that may change.
